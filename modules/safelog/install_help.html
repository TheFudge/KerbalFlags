How to Install

Step 1
Just copy the generator in a subfolder of your website.
Example: Webiste: www.example.com - subfolder: IPC
Your IPC wilbe located at http://www.example.com/IPC.

Step 2
Create space for the IPC in your database to save 3 different codes.
One is the crypted password.It will be made with creating the password.
The second two are the keys for the browser header and for every link, the second one is located in a cookie at the users computer.
